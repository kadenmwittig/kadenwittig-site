Up(LIFTING)

Year 2025
Format Narrative Short
Genre Drama · Sports
Runtime 7 min
Role Writer / Director / Editor

A young man starts down the long path towards physical fitness and self-improvement, with the goal of being able to do a single pull-up. Featuring gritty, high-contrast cinematography and a pulsing electronic score, the film tells its story through a tactile visual language rooted in the theory of montage.
