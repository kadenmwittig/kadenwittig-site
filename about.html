Kaden Wittig

Writer  ·  Director  ·  Video Production Professional

Kaden Wittig is an independent filmmaker based in Oklahoma City, OK, with a background spanning feature films, broadcast television, and commercial video. His work focuses on telling grounded, human stories through the medium of film. Starting his filmmaking journey through home videos at the age of 6, he has since directed multiple festival award-winning short films throughout high school and college, as well as the Telly Award-winning feature-length documentary Pillar: Beyond the Frontline (2026).

Kaden is a graduate of Tulsa Technology's TV Production program, has an A.A.S. in Liberal Arts, and graduated magna cum laude with a B.A. in Film and Media Studies and a minor in Creative Media Production from the University of Oklahoma.
