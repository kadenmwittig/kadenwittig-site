Where the Flowers Bloom

Year 2025
Format Narrative Short
Genre Drama · Coming-of-Age
Runtime 7 min
Role Director / Writer

A girl living in a run-down motel with her deaf grandmother experiences the joys and hardships of daily life in this rugged environment.
